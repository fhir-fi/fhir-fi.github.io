This year, the Nordic HL7 affiliates are concentrating their efforts on the Nordic Health Data Hackathons.

Nordic Health Data Hackathons

Hackathons are a great way to spread information on what's possible with modern technologies, as well as to validate the state and implementability of a technical specification or capabilities of a platform.

Nordic HL7 affiliates...

The first Nordic Health Data Hackathon was organized in Norway in November 2025, in connection with the EHiN conference. See more details on the Norwegian FHIR Hackathon 2025 page!

In 2026, there will be four Nordic Health Data Hackathons organized in Finland, Sweden, Denmark, and Norway. The intention is to make this a continuous series of hackathons.

The hackathons are organized in connection with trade events and conferences.

  • Finland, January 19-21, Radical Health Festival
  • Sweden, May 4-6, Vitalis
  • Denmark, (autumn 2026, event to be decided)
  • Norway, November 10, EHiN

Read more on the Nordic Health Data Hackathons page!
